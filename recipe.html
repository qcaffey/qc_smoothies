Creamy Avocado Banana Smoothie

Jump to Recipe
Category:
Hormone Balance
(522 ratings)

Description

This creamy avocado smoothie is easy to make, requiring just five ingredients, 1 blender, and 5 minutes. The base is frozen banana, which creates a creamy, naturally sweet base. Next comes the star ingredient: avocado! Full of fiber and healthy fats, avocado makes this smoothie creamy and dreamy and thick, almost like a milkshake.

Ingredients

  • 1 large frozen banana
  • 1/4 - 1/2 medium ripe avocado
  • 1 scoop plain or vanilla protein powder
  • 1 large handful greens of choice
  • 3/4 - 1 cup unsweetened plain almond milk
    (or any dairy-free milk)
  • 1 Tbsp frozen blueberries

Instructions

  1. To a high-speed blender, add frozen banana, avocado, protein powder of choice, greens, and dairy-free milk.
  2. Blend on high until creamy and smooth.
  3. Divide between serving glasses, top with frozen blueberries, and enjoy!
