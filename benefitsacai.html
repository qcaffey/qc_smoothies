Benefits of Superfoods

ACAI

Best Benefits: immune support, energy, antioxidants

Just like its berry-cousins, the blueberry and cranberry, acai is loaded with phytonutrients, fibre, vitamin C, vitamin A, essential amino acids and a range of trace minerals, especially manganese. Acai berries are also believed to help control appetite hormones, increase energy, prevent heart disease and support metabolic function.

You can find acai in the form of frozen smoothie packs, powder or capsules. Frozen smoothie packs are found in local grocery stores, but acai powder is great too and adds the most beautiful color to smoothies.
